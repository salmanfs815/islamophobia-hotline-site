Ten years ago, a group of concerned lawyers and legal organizations established the Islamophobia Legal Assistance Hotline in response to the rising tide of Islamophobia in Canada. Since its inception, the Hotline has been supported by numerous legal, civil society, and community organizations, enabling it to provide free legal advice and representation to individuals impacted by Islamophobia across British Columbia.  
  
Over the past decade, the Hotline has served hundreds of clients, facilitated numerous legal education workshops, and engaged in community events to support and empower British Columbians. We extend our sincere gratitude to the partner organizations and individuals who helped launch the Hotline, as well as to the dedicated volunteers who continue to sustain and strengthen this vital program. To donate to our program, visit: https://islamophobiahotline.org/contact-us/donate/
