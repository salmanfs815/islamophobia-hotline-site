Join the Islamophobia Legal Assistance Hotline and Salam Central for an engaging youth conference. Link in bio to register!  
  
This youth conference brings together young people to engage in meaningful conversations about Islamophobia and its impact on their daily lives. Through a series of interactive sessions, participants will explore key topics including religious accommodation, the intersections of anti-Black racism and Islamophobia, the unique experiences of Muslim women navigating public transit, and pathways to effective advocacy.  
The conference aims to create a safe and empowering space for youth to share their experiences, build knowledge of their rights, and develop practical skills to challenge discrimination in their schools and communities. By centering lived experiences and fostering dialogue, the event encourages participants to become informed and confident advocates for equity, inclusion, and justice.
